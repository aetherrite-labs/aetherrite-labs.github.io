Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Aetherrite Labs LLC ("Aetherrite Labs," "we," "us," or "our") is a California limited liability company that develops mobile software applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our website (aetherrite-labs.com) and our mobile applications, including Manawell.

Summary

We build our software to keep your data on your device. Our website sets no cookies and runs no analytics, and our apps do not send your personal data to us — we operate no servers that receive it. We do not sell or share your information, and we show no ads.

Our Website

aetherrite-labs.com does not collect personal information beyond what you voluntarily provide when you email us. We do not operate contact forms, user accounts, cookies, or third-party analytics on this website.

Manawell

Manawell is a usage monitor for AI assistant accounts. It displays usage figures that it fetches directly from an agent you run on your own computer, over a network connection you control (for example, a private Tailscale or local network). That connection is directly between your device and your own machine — Aetherrite Labs is never in the path, and we never receive your usage data or your credentials.

The details you enter to reach your own agent — a connection name, its host and port, and the pairing secret your agent generates — are stored only on your device. The usage figures Manawell retrieves are cached on your device, including in a private app group container that the Manawell widget reads, so the widget and Lock Screen Live Activity can display them. None of this data is transmitted to us or to any third party.

Manawell requires no account with Aetherrite Labs. Its Demo Mode shows synthetic, locally generated sample data and requires no connection at all. Manawell contains no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking software.

How We Use Information

Any information you send us directly (such as an email inquiry) is used solely to respond to your message. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because Manawell stores its data only on your device, you stay in control of it. You can remove a saved connection from within the app at any time, and deleting the app removes everything it stored, including any connection details and cached usage figures. If you have emailed us and would like us to delete that correspondence, contact us at support@aetherrite-labs.com and we will do so.

Children's Privacy

Our website and apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
