Frequently asked questions

How do I report a bug or issue with an app?
Email us at support@aetherrite-labs.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots or a screen recording are always helpful. We aim to respond within one business day.
How do I request a refund for an in-app purchase?
All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. Apple handles all billing and refund decisions.
Where can I find the privacy policy for your apps?
Our privacy policy is available at aetherrite-labs.com/privacy and explains exactly what each of our apps does — and doesn't do — with your data. A short summary also appears on each app's App Store product page.
How do I contact you about a business inquiry?
Send us an email at support@aetherrite-labs.com with the subject line "Business inquiry." We'll follow up within two business days.
Who is behind Aetherrite Labs?
Aetherrite Labs LLC is an independent iOS software studio incorporated in California in 2026. We're based in Murrieta, CA and focused on building native iOS apps with care and craft.
